About Me

Hello, my name is Gina and I am currently living in Los Angeles, California. I graduated from UC San Diego with a degree in Communications and minor in Law and Society, with a focus in Digital Marketing.
While pursuing my career in Marketing, I realized that I really enjoyed the creative aspects of my job, specifically digital media. I further explored digital media and graphic design, and it somehow led me to dabble around in coding/web development.
I have taken multiple classes on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through different resources (more familiar with HTML and CSS) and loved it. It was fascinating to see the code I write come to life. I have always been eager to learn more and can't wait to take on this new journey of web development and build upon my current skills and experiences!
